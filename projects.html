Procedural Terrain Synthesis for Sim-to-Real

Engineered a synthetic data generation pipeline using PyTorch, VGG-19 neural style transfer, and parametric Perlin noise to simulate natural seafloor textures learned from real sonar datasets, bridging the sim-to-real gap for underwater perception.
